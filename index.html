Algosys

Algosys is a team-driven project designed for DSA visualization, practical implementation, and MCQs to enhance learning. It provides an interactive platform for understanding algorithms through visual representation, hands-on coding, and self-assessment. Built with a passion for simplifying DSA concepts, Algosys aims to make learning more engaging and effective for students and programmers.

LearnNex

The e-learning platform named as "LearnNex" is an innovative team project, fully gamified experience designed specifically for kids, making learning fun and engaging. The platform integrates educational games, ensuring that children can grasp concepts while enjoying an interactive environment. It features animated video lectures to simplify complex topics, making them visually appealing and easy to understand. Additionally, the platform includes MCQ-based tests that are designed to keep students motivated, with attractive elements that encourage participation and self-improvement. By blending education with gamification, this project creates an immersive learning journey where children stay engaged, motivated, and excited to learn.

Hacathon Registration Website

My team and I developed a website called "Sinhgad Hackathon", designed to streamline the student registration and evaluation process for hackathons. This platform allows students to register online by submitting their ideas and necessary documents. Teachers, acting as administrators, can view real-time registrations, access all student details, and evaluate submitted ideas. They also have the functionality to shortlist students based on their submissions and download an Excel sheet of the selected participants. The website features two login systems: one for admins (teachers) to manage and monitor student registrations, and another for students to register for Phase 1 and Phase 2 of the hackathon. Built using React, PHP, CSS, and HTML, this system ensures a seamless, efficient, and organized hackathon registration and selection process.
